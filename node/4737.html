Pen pineapple apple pen

This song was so popular when just issued. Lyrics here. It makes you smile over a man that makes first steps in English language. Don't be confused with words that sound alike! Have a short hint and listen seriously:

P-P-A-P!

This is an abbreviation for the next sentence. This way song sharpens attention of listeners. Next it explains in detail its short meaning:

I have a pen, I have a apple
Uh! Apple-pen!

I have a pen, I have pineapple
Uh! Pineapple-pen!

Kids may learn how to combine parts of words and use it for create different words.

Next the song is playing the game with its own rules, creating multifunctional word "pen-pineapple-apple-pen". This is the end point of a lesson.
