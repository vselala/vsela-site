Robert Miles - Children

Кіт 1 26.02.2017 в 13:34

Einer schließt den Himmel auf,
kommt die liebe Sonne raus.

"Liebe, liebe Sonne"

 

The feature of this music is clear words that saving the story into the music. The tune revealed straight from the first notes.

The words flow into the scene playing with the actors. The words turn back into the music saying the message.

Robert Miles is a contemporary producer, composer and DJ, Wiki says. His club tracks "One & One", "Fable" are rotating on the radio. This music is made to be a background. The message of piano keys is soft and clear. It brings ideas to be a human wider and better.
