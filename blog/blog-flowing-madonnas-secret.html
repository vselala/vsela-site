The blog is flowing with Madonna's "Secret"

Кіт 1 30.01.2016 в 13:12

Incomprehensible line comes through your heart. The very exotic road to any place you never found. Come back you see an interpretation of past, look around, everything becomes alive. Moving forward you are playing as if the million strings touch all around getting back to you.

 

Madonna sings:
"Happiness lies in your own hand
It took me much too long to understand
How it could be
Until you shared your secret with me".

 

Contemporary american singer Madonna in song "Secret" offers a story about one woman's enlightenment. The thoughts are diving inside her. This is so intimately. The voice is very soft. Words flow slowly, we can hear the heartbeat in a very each word.

 

"Things haven't been the same
Since you came into my life
You found a way to touch my soul
And I'm never, ever, ever gonna let it go".

 

The story starts with a flashback to a moment she felt something new. Changing rhythm prompts us this is something she has not understood yet. The man she is talking to had changed her.

"Happiness lies in your own hand
It took me much too long to understand
How it could be
Until you shared your secret with me".

 

I think these words contain an assessment of Madonna's past life. Confessing own feelings, opening heart she is talking about the impression that one unidentified man had made on her.


Author: Lemmy-X

 

"Mmm mmm [skip for the first two times]
Something's comin' over, mmm mmm
Something's comin' over, mmm mmm
Something's comin' over me
My baby's got a secret".

 

The reason is the secret. The secret is intriguing the listeners. The secret is something you can get too. May be this is the formula, the knowledge? The word "secret" in this popular song is a form of expression. Singer wants to say us this is so simply when you look on it, you can pass it, but it is so hard to find. 

"You gave me back the paradise
That I thought I lost for good
You helped me find the reasons why
It took me by surprise that you understood
You knew all along
What I never wanted to say
Until I learned to love myself
I was never ever lovin' anybody else".

 

This song is about feelings. Madonna's life becomes so rich and happy with this secret, she is "never, ever, ever gonna let it go..." She has changed an attitude to herself, this is why she learned to love the others.

Thank you.
